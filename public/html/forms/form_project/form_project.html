Environmental factors are characteristics of environment like climatic conditions or chemical composition. You can add factors from the common list that is edited in a separate form outside the project. The factors can be linked to plots/samples.
Plots are sites with certain geographical location where organisms are collected. Alternatively, those can be experimental treatments or microcosms.
